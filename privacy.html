Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The Short Version

Your images never leave your device. All image processing happens locally in your web browser using JavaScript. We do not upload, store, or have access to any images you process with our tool.

Information We Collect

Like most websites, we collect basic analytics data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Browser type and device information
  • General geographic location (country level)
  • Referring website

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Image Processing

All image upscaling and processing happens entirely in your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and JavaScript. No image data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third-party service.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for essential site functionality and anonymous analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Google Fonts: Font loading for site design

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
