Our Top Picks

1. Best for Quick Online Use: UpscaleImage.AI

If you need a fast, free, no-signup online upscaler, UpscaleImage.AI is the clear winner. All processing happens in your browser, so your images never leave your device. No daily limits, no watermarks.

2. Best Quality: Real-ESRGAN / Upscayl

For the absolute best quality, Real-ESRGAN (and its desktop GUI, Upscayl) uses state-of-the-art AI models. The trade-off is speed — expect to wait 10-30 seconds per image depending on your hardware.

3. Best Online with Server AI: Bigjpg

Bigjpg offers strong AI upscaling with a generous free tier (20 images/day). Quality is excellent, especially for anime/illustration content. However, your images are uploaded to their servers.

Key Factors to Consider

  • Privacy: Do you need images processed locally? Choose client-side tools like UpscaleImage.AI or desktop apps like Upscayl.
  • Volume: Processing many images? Avoid tools with daily limits.
  • Quality vs Speed: Server-side AI models produce better quality but require upload and processing time.
  • Content Type: Some tools specialize in anime/illustrations (Waifu2x), while others excel at photos.

For most users, we recommend starting with a free client-side tool for quick tasks, and using a desktop AI upscaler for important projects where quality matters most.

Conclusion

The best image upscaler depends on your needs. For quick, private, unlimited use — try our free online upscaler. For the absolute best quality with no time pressure, install Upscayl on your desktop.
